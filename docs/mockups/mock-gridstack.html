Camp Planner — GridStack prototype

Row = day (one grid each), 15-min columns + a trailing “next-day” runway. Click a slot for details. Toggle ✎ Úpravy (top-left) to drag/resize; in edit mode double-click a slot for details. Drag/resize past 05:00 into the runway to split onto the next day. Overlap lanes / axis / row cap are computed by hand.
